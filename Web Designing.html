Web design is the process of creating websites. It encompasses several different aspects, including webpage layout, content production, and graphic design. While the terms web design and web development is often used interchangeably, web design is technically a subset of the broader category of web development.

A website is the medium through which viewers can access information or purchase products over the internet. Having a website enables a business to reach a wider market, or prospective client base. In today’s world, a website is undeniably essential in order to succeed in most industries. The competition and nature of the corporate world makes it crucial for any business to enhance its presence on a global basis. In this regard, website design is one of the most important things that you need to consider if you want to develop a website.
